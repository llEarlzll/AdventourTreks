ABOUT US

PHILOSOPHY

Adventour Treks is a team of ITE Students whose interest in the outdoors and has taken to far off destinations from mountains to distant seas. There is no greater feeling when you are out there experiencing nature. Our philosophy is that, as we travel we are able to find our individually passion and excitement when it comes to exploring nature, hence we are also trying to achieve and make a difference by promoting the advocacy in Climb for a Cause Activities in order to raise funds in campaigns and help our Mother Nature.
